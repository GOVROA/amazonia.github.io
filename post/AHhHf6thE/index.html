Aurora Twinkle attended the EU MicroCon in Ypres, Belgium

¬∑ 16-08-2023 ¬∑ # Diplomacy

The People's Revolutionary Repubulic of Yusienia was honored to participate in EU Microcon. The MicroCon is generally considered the most famous and important intermicronational conference. This EU MicroCon is the first MicroCon ever in Europe, and it is co-hosted by Flandrensis and Ladonia. It is hosted in Ypres, which located in the Flanders region of Belgium and has the world-famous Menin Gate.

On 10 August, President Aurora Twinkle fly from Athens to Bruselles, and visited this famous city, known as the capital of Europe.

The next day afternoon, Twinkle arrived in Ypres by train. She met with the leader of Coruscent and his family in a sushi restaurant. Coruscent is an Asian micronation and grabbed its name from Star Wars. Twinkle had dinner there, and together they meet with many micronational leaders: President Zar Antonov of Obscurium, Summi Imperatoria Christina of Cycoldia, and the delegation of Westarctica.

Twinkle visited the Lille Gate and war cemetery with Coruscent leader family. Twinkle said, the visit to the war cemetery reminds her of the song "It's a long way to Tipperary."

The attendees gathered together and attended The Last Post ceremony at Menin Gate. Then they meet and get a drink together.

In the morning of 12 August, the MicroCon started. Many attendees shared their amazing thoughts on the conference, and the representatives of the micronations showed their nations by their fascinating beautiful display tables.

Yusienia's display table shows QR codes to MicroWiki and Sovereign Yusienia, and have Yusienian badges, commemorative coins for this MicroCon, flags and some Yusienian ethnic food.

The MicroCon has established a communication platform for multilaterial meetings and provided a rare opportunity for leaders of various micronations to hold on bilaterial meetings.
