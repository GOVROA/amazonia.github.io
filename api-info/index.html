About Yusienia

\n

The Republic of Yusienia is an independent sovereign state located in Southern Europe and Mars. Yusienia have a tiny landlocked de-facto countrol zone with an area of 1556 square kilometres, located in north-east of Athens, Greece's capital, and it has no permanent population; Yusienia's citizens live on the territory of other macronations.
\nThe country is a unitary autocratic republic controlled by the princess of Yusienia, who also acts as the head of government.
\nYusienia's current territory claims a part of Asia Minor including Smyrna and Ephesus, the city and suburbs of Trabzon, and many islands, the Aegadian Islands, Chios, Lesbos, and Filfla, etc. , but has no ability to directly control these territories.

The Flag:

\n

The national flag of Yusienia consists four vertical stripes of gold and blue and a white square in the center with a blue pattern from the Emblem. Gold represents prosperity and honor, blue represents liberty and future, white represents egality and peace.
